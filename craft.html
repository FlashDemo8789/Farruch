The ateliers behind every glimmer of couture light.

Atelier network

Disciplines in concert with couture calendars.

Every brief is welcomed by a cohort of specialists who shepherd it from moodboard dramaturgy to the final fitting. Ancestral techniques meet material science, sustainable practice, and advanced machinery—without losing the intimacy of the hand.

Embroidery Studios

Needle to runway

Hand Embroidery

Over 300 lead embroiderers translate couture briefs into zardozi, ari, tambour, bullion, and micro pavé compositions, balancing opulence with Farruch’s hallmark lightness.

Each motif is rehearsed on muslin before silk, tulle, velvet, or leather is introduced—ensuring embroidery clings to the silhouette exactly as imagined.

Machine Embroidery

Hand-guided machinery enables dimensional motifs, metallic weaves, and sequined gradients at couture precision. Technicians blend digital mapping with hand embellishment so the soul of the stitch remains unmistakably human.

Tailoring

Master cutters drape, pad, and balance every silhouette so embroidery flows without interruption. Corsetry, bodices, and fluid trains are engineered to withstand rehearsals, red carpet flashes, and museum lighting.

Production & Finishing

End-to-end excellence

Accessories, Shoes & Bags

Accessory ateliers craft minaudières, heels, veils, and jewellery panels to echo the primary garment. Techniques shift from rigid metalwork to soft net appliqué so every accent feels like an extension of the couture narrative.

Artwork & Design

Art directors sketch in gouache and translate motifs into digital blueprints using CAD suites. Every artwork arrives with stitch counts, bead charts, and placement guides for seamless interpretation across ateliers.

Dyeing & Printing

Digital and screen printing labs operate alongside azo-free dyeing suites to deliver exact colour gradations. Fabrics are stress-tested for embellishment compatibility before entering embroidery floors.

Quality Control

Quality specialists examine each panel under runway-grade lighting, checking tension, backing, and drape. Inspection marks are recorded in digital dossiers for maison records.

Despatch

Dedicated logistics partners manage customs, climate-controlled freight, and hand-carry services so runway looks, red-carpet ensembles, and exhibition pieces arrive immaculate and on schedule.

Knowledge & Liaison

Preserve & communicate

Archive

Over 20,000 catalogued swatches chronicle a century of Farruch savoir-faire. Designers consult the archive to revive techniques, study colour stories, and reference construction notes preserved alongside every sample.

Merchandising Team

Dedicated merchandisers serve as the maison’s voice throughout each commission—documenting every fitting, coordinating cross-continental touchpoints, and safeguarding timelines from first briefing to final reveal.
