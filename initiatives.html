Beyond couture

Responsibility is woven into every commission.

Ethics charters, artisan empowerment, and environmental stewardship guide the maison. Scholarships, training programs, and circular practices ensure Farruch’s craft endures for future generations.

Central Saint Martins Residency

Each season, Farruch underwrites a graduating designer’s final collection—pairing them with mentors across Mumbai and Paris, opening our studios, and funding atelier residencies to translate their thesis into couture form.

Teach · Empower · Employ

Our women-led embroidery school offers paid training to underrepresented artisans. Graduates transition directly into maison roles with mentorship pathways, helping rebalance a craft historically dominated by men.

UTTHAN Collective

As a founding member of UTTHAN, Farruch works alongside leading luxury groups to safeguard embroidery karigars—championing dignified wages, safe ateliers, and global recognition for artisan brilliance.

Sustainability commitments

  • Solar arrays powering ateliers and administrative floors at Farruch Tower.
  • Rainwater capture that replenishes the surrounding groundwater table.
  • Plastic reduction programmes—50% eliminated by 2019, tracking toward 100% by 2025.
  • Circularity initiatives that transform embroidery offcuts into packaging, décor, and donations for design schools.
Ethical framework

Since incorporating in 2004, Farruch has embedded an ethics charter into every contract. Each employee and associate signs the code, ensuring respect, transparency, and safety form the backbone of our operations.

Environmental milestones—solar energy, rainwater harvesting, circular material use—are audited annually, keeping the maison accountable to the communities and ecosystems we inhabit.
