Heritage 1904 — Present

The maison and its lineage

Founded in 1904, Farruch began as the Zariwala family’s pledge to guard India’s most intricate embroidery disciplines while speaking fluently to Parisian couture. Six generations later, the maison continues to answer couture briefs with the assurance of artisans who carry muscle memory in every stitch.

Each commission is orchestrated from Farruch Tower in Mumbai and translated through liaison studios in London, Milan, Paris, and New York—ensuring designers feel the maison’s presence from concept to final promenade.

Artistry

The anatomy of our ateliers

Hand Embroidery

Master embroiderers draft every stitch path by hand, translating briefs into zardozi, tambour, bullion, and micro pavé sequences that carry the maison’s signature lightness.

Material Alchemy

Textile researchers test silk organza, metallic chiffon, and bio-based fibres for drape, pigment absorption, and embellishment compatibility before the first stone is set.

Tailoring Architecture

Pattern masters build scaffolding beneath the embroidery, ensuring corsetry, fluid gowns, and molded silhouettes honour the designer’s line on runway and red carpet.

Bijoux & Accents

Dedicated artisans craft jewellery panels, minaudières, footwear, and veils so that every commission arrives with a fully harmonised constellation of accessories.

Bespoke Commissions

From concept board to couture finale

Each engagement opens with an immersion. Creative directors gather in Farruch Tower while our team scripts tactile essays of lace, light, and silhouette. Swatch caskets and digital lookbooks travel in tandem so every choice is felt as much as it is seen.

Prototypes move between Mumbai, Paris, and New York for fittings, while dedicated merchandisers steward timing, hand-carry deliveries, and ensure the couture reveal lands precisely on cue.

Commitments

Ensuring the craft endures

Central Saint Martins Residency

Each season we underwrite a CSM graduate’s couture collection, pairing them with Farruch mentors in Mumbai and Paris to translate sketches into debut-ready embroideries.

Teach · Empower · Employ

Our women-led embroidery school offers paid training to underrepresented artisans, culminating in full-time atelier placements and ongoing mentorship.

Responsible Luxury

Solar arrays, rainwater harvesting, material recycling, and our UTTHAN commitments anchor Farruch’s promise to honour the planet as reverently as the craft.
