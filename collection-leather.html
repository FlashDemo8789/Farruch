Artisanal Leather

Securing handcrafted leather luggage, briefcases, and accessories born from centuries-old Tuscan craftsmanship.

Sourcing & Provenance: A Legacy of Hide & Hardware

Our leather acquisition network runs deep through the traditional tanneries of Florence, Italy. We identify makers who adhere to meticulous vegetable-tanning processes and bespoke brass hardware finishing to ensure goods age beautifully.

Because high-end leather is susceptible to atmospheric fluctuations, Farruch strictly enforces climate and humidity-controlled logistics from the workbench to your private vault. Our team manages all CITES documentation if exotic skins are requested.
