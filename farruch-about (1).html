Our Story

Founded in 1994 by Akram Khan, a visionary entrepreneur with a passion for luxury goods and global trade, Farruch Exports began in Bangalore, specializing in sourcing rare textiles and artisanal crafts from across Asia for high-end European fashion houses.

What started as a specialized service for a select clientele quickly evolved into a comprehensive luxury import and export operation as word spread about our meticulous attention to detail, extensive network of connections, and uncompromising commitment to quality and authenticity.

Today, Farruch Exports has grown into a premier luxury goods trading company with offices in key fashion and luxury centers around the world. Our reputation for excellence and discretion has made us the trusted partner for prestigious retailers, luxury brands, private collectors, and discerning clients seeking exceptional products and seamless international trade services.

Our Journey

Explore the key milestones in Farruch Exports' evolution from a boutique operation to a global luxury import-export leader.

1994

Foundation

Farruch Exports was established in Bangalore, initially focusing on sourcing luxury textiles from Asia for European fashion houses.

2000

First International Office

Expanded operations with the opening of our first international office in Milan, Italy, strengthening our European presence.

2005

Service Expansion

Broadened our service offerings to include comprehensive import/export solutions across all luxury product categories.

2010

Global Network

Established offices in key luxury markets including New York, Paris, and Dubai, creating a truly global operation.

2015

Digital Transformation

Implemented advanced logistics tracking and client management systems to enhance service efficiency and transparency.

2020

Sustainability Initiative

Launched our commitment to sustainable luxury trade practices and responsible sourcing principles.

2025

Current Era

Today, Farruch Exports stands as a premier global luxury import-export company with an unmatched reputation for excellence, discretion, and expertise.
