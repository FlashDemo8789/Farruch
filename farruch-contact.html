Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our luxury import and export services.

What types of luxury products do you import and export?

Farruch Exports specializes in a wide range of luxury products, including fine jewelry and gemstones, luxury textiles and fabrics, artisanal leather goods, crystal and decorative arts, luxury timepieces, rare collectibles, and other high-end items. We work with prestigious manufacturers, artisans, and luxury houses across the globe to source exceptional products for our discerning clients.

How do you ensure the authenticity of luxury products?

We maintain rigorous authentication protocols for all products we handle. Our team of specialists with expertise in specific luxury categories conducts thorough examinations of each item. Additionally, we work directly with authorized manufacturers and artisans, maintain detailed documentation of provenance, and employ advanced authentication technologies when necessary. Every product is accompanied by appropriate certificates of authenticity and detailed documentation.

What import/export services do you provide?

Our comprehensive services include product sourcing and procurement, customs clearance and documentation, regulatory compliance management, logistics coordination for secure transportation, market research and strategy development for exporters, distribution network access, and private consultation for unique requirements. We handle all aspects of the import/export process to ensure a seamless experience for our clients.

How do you handle customs and compliance requirements?

Our customs specialists maintain up-to-date knowledge of international trade regulations and luxury goods-specific requirements in all major markets. We prepare and process all necessary documentation, manage duties and taxes, obtain required permits and licenses, and ensure compliance with all applicable regulations. Our expertise in navigating complex customs procedures helps prevent delays and compliance issues for high-value luxury items.

Can you source specific luxury items based on client requirements?

Yes, our private consultation service specializes in sourcing specific luxury items based on detailed client requirements. Through our extensive global network of relationships with prestigious makers, we can access rare, limited edition, and exclusive items that may not be available through conventional channels. We also arrange bespoke commissions with renowned artisans and luxury houses for truly unique pieces.

What security measures do you implement for transporting valuable items?

We implement comprehensive security measures including specialized handling protocols for high-value items, secure packaging with tamper-evident seals, trusted transportation partners with experience in luxury goods, discrete routing with minimal transfer points, real-time tracking systems, and comprehensive insurance coverage. Our approach ensures that valuable items are transported with the highest level of security and care from origin to destination.
