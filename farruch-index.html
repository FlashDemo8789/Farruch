Farruch creates exquisite handcrafted embellishments, embroidery, and textiles featured on international runways and red carpets by the most prestigious fashion designers.

Discover Our Artistry

Heritage of Excellence

For over three decades, Farruch has been at the forefront of luxury handwork, preserving ancient techniques while innovating for the modern fashion industry. Our artisans combine centuries-old traditions with contemporary vision to create extraordinary pieces that define haute couture.

Every stitch, bead, and embellishment is meticulously placed by master craftspeople who have dedicated their lives to perfecting their art. This unwavering commitment to excellence has made Farruch the premier partner for the world's most discerning fashion designers.

Hand Embroidery

Couture Finishes

Trusted by Elite Designers

Our handwork graces the runway collections and red carpet creations of the world's most prestigious fashion houses
